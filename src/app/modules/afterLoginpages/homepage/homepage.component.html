When someone you love needs care at home, trust matters.

At Caring Roots Home Care Inc., we understand how difficult it can be for families to make care decisions for a parent, spouse, or loved one. Our mission is simple: to provide dependable, compassionate home care that families can truly count on.

We believe people deserve to remain safely and comfortably in their own homes for as long as possible. Our caregivers provide respectful, person-centred support designed around each individual’s needs, routines, and preferences—while offering families peace of mind.

Local Care, Rooted in the Community

Caring Roots Home Care Inc. proudly serves Miramichi and surrounding communities, supporting families throughout the region with reliable, in-home care. Our local focus allows us to respond quickly, communicate clearly, and deliver care that reflects the values of the communities we serve.

  • Personal care (bathing, dressing, grooming)

  • Medication reminders

  • Meal preparation and light housekeeping

  • Mobility and transfer assistance

  • Respite care for family caregivers

  • Companionship and emotional support

  • Post-hospital or recovery support
